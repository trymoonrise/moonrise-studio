BLINDS

Horizontal slices unlock on scroll

MOTION IS STRUCTURE
STRUCTURE IS RHYTHM
RHYTHM IS REVEAL
REVEAL IS CONTROL
CONTROL IS SCROLL

Each blind expands from opposite edges — odd rows from the right, even from the left — so the sentence stitches itself together.

NO FADE. NO BLUR.
ONLY SHUTTERS.
OPENING ON DEMAND.
CLOSING ON EXIT.

Progress is bidirectional — scroll back and the blinds slam shut again.
