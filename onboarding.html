Introduction

Moonrise is an AI-powered studio that allows you find local businesses, and generate a polished website in 1 swipe. All in one platform.

How it works

Here’s the whole loop — from finding a business to getting paid.

  1. 1
    Find a business

    Use Business Finder to search local businesses that need a website.

  2. 2
    Build & set a price

    Generate the site in one swipe. Set what the owner will pay to unlock it.

  3. 3
    Publish with a watermark

    The live link is a preview. The watermark stays on until they pay, so they can see it before unlocking.

  4. 4
    Send the link

    Share that preview with the owner. You don’t collect money yourself.

  5. 5
    They unlock with Stripe

    They tap the watermark, pay once, and it comes off. The site stays live.

  6. 6
    You get paid

    They show up in My clients. Paid sites can’t be deleted. Then find the next one and repeat.
