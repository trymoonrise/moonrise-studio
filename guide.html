What Moonrise is

Moonrise lets you build websites for local businesses for free and get paid when they unlock the site on the watermark checkout. Find a lead, generate with AI, customize, publish with the watermark, and share the live site link.

  • Free to build - you create and preview sites at no cost to the business owner.
  • They pay on the watermark - published sites stay watermarked until the owner pays through Moonrise on the live site.
  • You never charge them - do not collect cash, Venmo, invoices, or any payment outside Moonrise.
  • You keep 80% - @moonrise takes 20% of each go-live payment for Stripe, hosting, and platform costs.
  • Paid clients show up under My Clients; your income tracks on the Dashboard.

How you get paid (important)

Creators do not charge business owners. Your job is to build, publish, and share the live site link. The owner pays only through the Moonrise watermark on the deployed website — that is the sole checkout for going live.

When they pay, the watermark is removed automatically, the site stays live, and you receive 80% of the sale (@moonrise keeps 20%). Set the unlock price in the editor before you publish.

Charging the owner directly - or asking them to pay outside the watermark - violates Moonrise Terms and may result in permanent ban, withheld payouts, site removal, and other severe consequences. See Terms of Service.

Your creator loop

  1. Find Use Business Finder to search local leads, or paste a Google Maps link in Builder.
  2. Generate Slide to generate in Business Finder or fill details in Builder, then edit in Editor. Generation is free.
  3. Polish Edit copy, layout, contact form, and domain settings in Builder. Preview desktop and mobile before you share.
  4. Sell Send the live site link. The owner pays on the site — not to you.
  5. Get paid When they pay through the watermark, you keep 80% (@moonrise keeps 20%). Add payout methods in Settings and track income on the Dashboard.
