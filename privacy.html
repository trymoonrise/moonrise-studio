Privacy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Overview

Moonrise Studio (“we”, “us”) helps operators find businesses, build sites, and manage client work. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as your email, username, and profile settings.
  • Workspace activity including leads you search, projects you create, and sites you publish.
  • Payment and payout details needed to process earnings and withdrawals.
  • Support messages you send through Help or email.
  • Basic technical data such as device type, browser, and usage logs for security and reliability.

How we use information

  • Provide, secure, and improve Moonrise Studio.
  • Authenticate you and personalize your workspace.
  • Process payouts and respond to support requests.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, and violations of our Terms.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run the product (for example hosting, authentication, and payments), when required by law, or with your direction when you publish a client site or share work with a client.

Retention & security

We keep information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, but no system is perfectly secure.

Your choices

You may update profile details in Settings, request account deletion by contacting us, and opt out of non-essential communications. Some processing is required to operate your account.
